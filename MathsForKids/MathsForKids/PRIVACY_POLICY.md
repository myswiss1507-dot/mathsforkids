# Privacy Policy for MathsForKids

**Last Updated:** November 28, 2025

## Overview
MathsForKids is a children's educational math game designed for ages 2-12. We are committed to protecting your privacy and your child's privacy.

## Data Collection
**We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.**

All data is stored locally on your device only:
- Child's name (optional, stored locally)
- Age and gender selection (stored locally)
- Game scores and session history (stored locally)
- Language preference (stored locally)

## Data Usage
The locally stored data is used solely to:
- Personalize the gaming experience
- Track progress and high scores
- Generate parent reports on your device

## Third-Party Services
This app does NOT use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Data Sharing
We do NOT share any data with third parties. All data remains on your device.

## Children's Privacy
This app is designed for children and complies with children's privacy regulations. Since no data leaves the device, there is no risk of unauthorized access to your child's information.

## Contact
For questions about this privacy policy, please contact: support@mathsforkids.com

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app update notes.
