Privacy Policy for Maths For Kids

Last Updated: November 29, 2025

Overview

Maths For Kids ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our educational mathematics application for children.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

All data generated by the App is stored locally on your device and never leaves your device. We do not have access to any information you enter into the App.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following information locally on your device using iOS standard storage (UserDefaults):

  • User Profile: Name, age, gender, and preferred language
  • Game Progress: Scores, session history, and performance statistics
  • High Scores: Best scores for each difficulty level

This data:

  • Remains on your device only
  • Is never transmitted to any server
  • Can be completely removed by uninstalling the App
  • Is not backed up to iCloud

No Third-Party Services

The App does not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Cloud storage or backup services
  • Any other third-party data collection tools

Children's Privacy

This App is designed specifically for children aged 2-10 years. We take children's privacy seriously:

  • We do not collect any personal information from children
  • We do not require account creation or login
  • All data remains on the device and under parental control
  • The App complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

Parental Controls

Parents have full control over all data in the App:

  • View performance reports through the Parent Report feature
  • Delete all data by uninstalling the App
  • No data persists after uninstallation

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device using iOS standard storage mechanisms, the security of your data is protected by:

  • iOS device security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID)
  • iOS app sandboxing (data cannot be accessed by other apps)

No Internet Connection Required

The App functions completely offline and does not require an internet connection. No data is ever transmitted over the internet.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any data, there is no data for us to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over all App data through your device settings and can remove all data by uninstalling the App.
